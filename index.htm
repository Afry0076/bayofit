About BayoFit
B
The founder
18 · Semi-pro footballer · Coeliac

BayoFit was built from personal experience. I'm 18, just finished my A-levels, and spent years chasing fitness goals that felt completely out of reach — multiple PTs, more diet plans than I can count, and more failed attempts than I'd like to admit.

"The problem wasn't the effort. It was finding something honest about what was actually realistic — and something that understood I was trying to become a better athlete, not just look better in photos."

I have coeliac disease and play semi-professional football. BayoFit exists because I couldn't find what I needed. So I built it.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

BayoFit is a sport-specific fitness platform operated as a sole trader in the UK. Contact: bayofit@outlook.com

2. What we collect

  • Full name and email address
  • Age, height, weight, and gender
  • Sport, fitness goals, and training experience
  • Dietary requirements including coeliac disease
  • Training availability and schedule preferences
  • Subscription and billing information via Stripe
  • Session logs, progress data, and performance notes
  • League data, challenge submissions, and proof photos

3. Why we collect it

To generate your personalised training plan, calculate calorie and macro targets, process subscription payments, enable team leagues, send account notifications, and send marketing emails where you have opted in.

4. Lawful basis

Under UK GDPR: Contract — to deliver the service; Legitimate interests — to improve the platform; Consent — for marketing emails, withdrawable at any time.

5. Health data

Height, weight, dietary requirements, and health information are special category data under UK GDPR. We collect this solely to provide personalised fitness and nutrition guidance.

6. Third parties

Stripe — payment processing. Firebase by Google — secure cloud storage. Mailchimp — email marketing. We do not sell your data. All providers have Data Processing Agreements in place.

7. Your rights

Access, correct, delete, restrict, object, or port your data. Email bayofit@outlook.com — we respond within 30 days.

8. Children

BayoFit is for users 13 and over. Users under 16 require parental consent per UK GDPR.

9. Complaints

Lodge a complaint with the UK ICO at ico.org.uk.
